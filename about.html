About Us

Founded by John Cruz in 1995, Ace Engineering has been the trusted partner of industry and governments worldwide. We have delivered more than 5000 projects in 25 countries. Today, Ace Engineering is one of the most respected global engineering and construction management companies. Through close cooperation with our clients and all stake-holders, we strive to achieve our mission: To deliver high-quality landmark projects that have significant positive impacts to economic progress.

Ace Engineering is known for its commitment to sustainability and innovation. We have received over 100 awards worldwide and we strive for continuous improvement. Our efforts for sustainable engineering have been recognized by governments and local communities. Ace Engineering believes in the balance between technology and environment and we never compromise.

We strongly believe in and promote ethics, safety, quality, people, culture, relationships, innovation and sustainability. And we abide by integrity, respect, collaboration, trust, and delivery. These are the foundations of our service.
